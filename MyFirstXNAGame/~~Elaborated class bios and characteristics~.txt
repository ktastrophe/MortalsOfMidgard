Medicine Man/Woman

These mysterious apocetharies hail from the remotest reaches of Lappland. 

Secondary characteristics: 

A subsistence existence has slowed their metabolism, meaning they need not eat as often as the other classes--but the side effects of food take longer to kick in, too. Armed with a knowledge of anatomy, they can sometimes disable a foe's natural defenses with a crippling blow.

Weapon: Leister (3-pronged fishing spear)
